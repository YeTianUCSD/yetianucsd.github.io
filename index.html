About Me

I am a graduate research student at National Tsing Hua University, majoring in Electrical Engineering. I am a member of Vision Science Lab where I am advised by Prof. Min Sun.

My research interests are in the area of Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Embedded System, especially in Deep Learning. My current research project focuses on learning human intention from object and motion interaction with wearable devices.
